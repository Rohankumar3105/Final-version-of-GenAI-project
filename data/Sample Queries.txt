Below are **clean, ready-to-use RAG prompts** you can use to query the policies you provided.
These prompts are designed to retrieve precise sections, clauses, definitions, workflows, examples, and obligations from your documents.

# **General RAG Query Prompts (for any policy)**

### **1. Policy Lookup**

* *“According to the <policy_name>, what does the document say about <topic>?”*
* *“Summarize the section related to <topic> in the <policy_name>.”*

### **2. Clause / Sub-Clause Retrieval**

* *“Retrieve all clauses under section <number> from the <policy_name>.”*
* *“What does clause <number> state in the <policy_name>?”*

### **3. Comparison Queries**

* *“Compare the requirements for employees and managers in the <policy_name>.”*
* *“How does the policy differentiate between <concept A> and <concept B>?”*

### **4. Workflow & Process Queries**

* *“Explain the step-by-step workflow for <process> as defined in the <policy_name>.”*
* *“What actions must an employee take before <action> according to the policy?”*

### **5. Responsibilities Queries**

* *“What responsibilities does the policy assign to HR / managers / employees?”*
* *“Retrieve all duties listed for managers in the <policy_name>.”*

### **6. Definitions Extraction**

* *“What is the definition of <term> in the <policy_name>?”*
* *“List all defined terms in the policy and their meanings.”*

### **7. Case Study Queries**

* *“What does the case study in the <policy_name> illustrate about <topic>?”*
* *“Retrieve any examples related to <topic>.”*

### **8. FAQ Retrieval**

* *“Retrieve FAQs related to <subject> in the <policy_name>.”*
* *“What does the policy FAQ say about <question/topic>?”*
